The PlumbXpert app is coming soon, and here are 10 features for the app you should watch out for:
    Easy booking process

    The app would have a user-friendly interface that allows you to quickly and effortlessly book a plumber. No complicated steps, just a few taps and you're good to go!

    Detailed plumber profiles

    Each plumber on the app would have a detailed profile showcasing their experience, qualifications, and customer reviews. This way, you can easily find the right plumber for your specific needs.

    Availability calendar

    The app would display the availability of plumbers, so you can see when they are free to take on your job. This helps you find a plumber who can accommodate your schedule.

    Real-time jobs status updates

    You'll receive real-time updates on the status of your job. From confirming the booking to the plumber's arrival time, you'll always be in the loop.

    In-app chat with plumbers

    The app would have a built-in chat feature that allows you to communicate directly with the plumber. You can ask questions, provide additional details, or get updates on the job progress.

    Secure payment options

    The app would offer secure payment options, ensuring that your financial information is protected. You can pay for the service within the app, making the process convenient and hassle-free.

    Ratings and reviews from previous customers

    You can check the ratings and reviews left by previous customers for each plumber. This helps you make an informed decision and choose a plumber with a good reputation.

    24/7 emergency service request

    In case of plumbing emergencies, the app would have a dedicated feature to request emergency services. You can quickly get assistance from a plumber, even outside regular business hours.

    Service history and invoices

    The app would keep a record of your service history and provide digital invoices. This allows you to easily track your past jobs and keep organized records for future reference.

    Push notifications for appointment reminders

    The app would send you push notifications as appointment reminders. You'll never miss a scheduled appointment and can plan your day accordingly.

These features would make the app user-friendly, efficient, and provide a seamless experience for booking and managing plumbing services.
